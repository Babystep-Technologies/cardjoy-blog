## Why start a card in Slack?

Slack is where your team already gathers, so it's the most natural place to rally everyone around a card. Instead of emailing a link and hoping people notice, you start the card right in the channel, and teammates add their own note, memory, or well-wish in a few clicks. Every voice lands in one beautiful card — no chasing, no copy-and-paste.

Ready to make one? Add CardJoy to your Slack workspace, start a group card, and gather your team around a card worth signing.
