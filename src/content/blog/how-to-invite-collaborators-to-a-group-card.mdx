## Why invite others?

The whole point of a group card is that it's a shared effort. Instead of chasing people down over text and copying their words in yourself, you send one link and let everyone add their own note, memory, or well-wish directly. Each person writes in their own voice, and it all lands in one beautiful card.

Ready to gather your group? Create a group card in the CardJoy app, share the invite, and watch the messages roll in.
