## Why group cards?

A group card gives everyone a shared space to leave a note, a memory, or a well-wish — all collected in one beautiful card instead of a scattered thread of texts. Once it's ready, you send the finished card to the recipient and let the messages speak for themselves.

Ready to make one? Try it yourself inside the CardJoy app, and gather your group around a card worth signing.
