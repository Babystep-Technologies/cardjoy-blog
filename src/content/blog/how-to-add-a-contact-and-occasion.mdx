The hardest part of sending a card on time is remembering the date at all. CardJoy fixes that: save the people you care about as contacts, add the occasions that matter — a birthday, a work anniversary, a milestone — and let CardJoy remind you when it's time to celebrate.

Watch the short, step-by-step demo above — just click to play — and you'll see how to add a contact, set up a recurring occasion, and turn a one-time card into a habit of showing up.

## Why save contacts and occasions?

Recognition works best when it's consistent, and consistency is hard to keep in your head. By storing a contact and their occasions in CardJoy, you turn "I should send something" into a gentle nudge that arrives right on time — so the moment never slips past you again.

Ready to get ahead of the next celebration? Add your first contact in the CardJoy app, set the occasion, and let CardJoy handle the reminders.
